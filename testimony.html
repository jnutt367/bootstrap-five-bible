My Testimony: Breaking Free, Building Hope

In the echoes of Revelation, a voice proclaimed, "They triumphed over him by the blood of the Lamb and by the word of their testimony;"... These words, a beacon of hope, resonate with my journey.

I am Jason Nutt. Once, I was deeply entrenched in the throes of alcoholism, a life riddled with lies and self-indulgence. I wore the cloak of irresponsibility, blinding myself to my duties. I frequented rehab, a carousel of failed attempts until, by the grace of the Holy Spirit, I found redemption.

Transformed, I embraced a new purpose. Nearly 15 years ago now, I embarked on a path of sobriety, a long, winding, path, that had many pitfalls and moments of despair and lonliness.

I am not proud to say, but say boldly to display the miraculaous change, that I have walked the lonely roads of the 4am street walker. looking for more...more of whatever it was I was looking for.

What that more was, I would not have ever been able to tell you, just something that was missing and nothing I could find on my own.

After so many failed attempts at rehab, one faithful night at the Salvation Army Adult Recovery Center for men, I got down on these kness of mine and said, "Well, If this "Jesus" is who everyone is trying to tell me that He is, YOU are going to have to MAKE me believe it. I don't believe you. You'll need to change my mind..."

Well..Let me tell you! The Lord is faithful, He did exactly that AND so much more!

Today, through divine guidance and tireless dedication, I've birthed a Bible application. Every word meticulously transcribed, each book coded, all freely available worldwide, devoid of advertisements.

I HIGHLY ADVISE you to take a second and do this, all you have to do is ask, He will do the rest.

Grateful for Your grace, I implore those touched by this mission to join the journey. The app, a testament to Your spirit within me, progresses from humble origins to its current form.

Witness the evolution, a testament to perseverance inspired by Your divine guidance.

Once lost in the depths of alcoholism, self-pity, and spiritual turmoil, I am now found in the grace of Jesus. His life, death, and resurrection have transformed me, saved by the enduring power of God's Holy Spirit.

This app stands as a testament, coded, published, and deployed by Your servant: Jason Daniel Nutt.

"All glory to GOD, who is able, through His mighty power at work within us, to accomplish infinitely more than we ask or imagine" Ephesians 3:20
